About Us

Who are we?

We are a small and independent bakery located at the heart of Madison. We want to connect with people by sharing our most creative and delicious products made from scratch. We bake a variety of food ranging from bread to confectionaries. The founder, Honda, was once a production assistant at an animation studio. However, he felt like he wasn't living his dream. As a kid, Honda would dream of opening a bakery but felt like it was an impossible feat at the time. Things would change as he would quit to chase his dream to open up a bakery in Madison.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to share our creations with the community that Honda grew up in. Honda was a great baker as a child, he could bake almost anything he could put his mind to. Honda was also a member of a bakery club back in high school, he would run bake sales. Most of the people that bought from the bake sale were mostly satisfied, which made Honda happy. He would imagine what it would be like if he could run a bakery for an entire community versus a school setting.

Our Goal

Our goal is to satify everyone who buy what we've crafted. We put every effort into our craft to make sure you are feeling satisfied. Whether that would be cinnamon rolls, cookies, muffins, etc., our goal is still the same. We encourage everyone to spread the word about Bake Shop, so that we can give back to the community!
